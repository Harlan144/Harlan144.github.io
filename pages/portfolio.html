Identifying images in the biology literature that are problematic for people with a color-vision deficiency


Nov 29, 2023 (bioapps.byu.edu/colorblind_image_tester)

For this paper, I worked under Dr. Piccolo to determine how often scientific images are colorblind-friendly and create tools to improve accessibility in biological literature. I helped annotate 6,000 images from the eLife journal. I then used this dataset to train a convolutional neural network to accurately predict whether images are colorblind-friendly or not. I created a website using Node.js to deploy the model and allow users to test images.

Stevens HP, Winegar CV, Oakley AF, Piccolo SR. Identifying images in the biology literature that are problematic for people with a color-vision deficiency. BioRxiv. 2023 Nov 29. DOI: 10.1101/2023.11.29.569308. [Preprint, Submitted for review].

Tolman Electronic Parameter Predictions from a Machine Learning Model Provide Insight into Phosphine Ligand Electronic Effects

Oct 10, 2023 (tepid.chem.byu.edu)

For this paper, I worked under Dr. Ess to optimize a machine-learning model that predicts Tolman Electronic Parameters -- a measure of a ligand's electron withdrawing capacity. Along with this, I developed a website to deploy the model, generated a new database of phosphines, and authored and edited the manuscript.

Stevens HP, Olsen J, Kirkland JK, Ess DH. Tolman Electronic Parameter Predictions from a Machine Learning Model Provide Insight into Phosphine Ligand Electronic Effects. ChemRxiv. 2023 Oct 10. DOI: 10.26434/chemrxiv-2023-wjnpj. [Preprint, Submitted for review].

Short Sequence Aligner Benchmarking for Chromatin Research

Sep 14, 2023

I worked with PhD candidate John Carter to benchmark different aligner algorithms used in chromatin research. My main responsibility was to write custom Python scripts that analyze how frequently aligner algorithms fail to map reads to their biological locations and determine the impact of this on downstream analysis for chromatin structure experiments (ie, ATAC-seq, CHiP-Seq, and MNase-seq experiments).

Carter JC, Stevens H, Ridge PG, Johnson SM. Short Sequence Aligner Benchmarking for Chromatin Research. International Journal of Molecular Sciences. 2023 Sep 14. DOI: 10.3390/ijms241814074.

Changes in Attitudes and Barriers to Seasonal Influenza Vaccination from 2007 to 2023

Sep 11, 2023

This study investigated how factors and barriers to flu vaccination among college students have changed over the past 16 years. My main role was to clean and analyze survey data using Pandas and R. Among our findings, we noted that the Covid-19 pandemic has changed vaccine attitudes, and vaccine fatigue has been a large contributor. Additionally, political affiliation has become a predictor of flu vaccine uptake.

Skyles TJ, Stevens HP, Obray AM, Jensen JL, Miner DS, Bodily RJ, Nielson BU, Poole BD. Changes in Attitudes and Barriers to Seasonal Influenza Vaccination from 2007 to 2023. Journal of Community Health. 2023 Sep 11. DOI: 10.1007/s10900-023-01277-7.

Genetic Modification of NMAD-1 Demethylase in C. elegans to Affect Longevity

We are investigating the impact of nmad-1 (a DNA m6dA demethylase) on aging in C. elegans (a nematode worm). We used RNAi to knockdown nmad-1 expression, and we are working on overexpressing it through microinjection. We hope that our research will link m6dA-methylaton to longevity and eventually lead to novel, epigenetic therapeutics.

Harlan Stevens, John Carter, Steven Johnson. Genetic Modification of NMAD-1 Demethylase in C. elegans to Affect Longevity. Utah Conference of Undergraduate Research. Feb 2023.

Searching the Scriptures Based on Semantic Similarity

As a pet project with some friends, I used a machine-learning-based approach to find semantic similarities between scriptural texts. We fine-tuned a transformer-based model on scriptural texts and calculated the cosine-similarity between all verses to find novel insights into what scriptures are similar. I made this tool into a website, where users can upload verses and the NLP model returns the five most semantically similar verses. We hope this work will help facilitate dialogue between religious groups and enhance scripture study. More progress on this is forthcoming!
